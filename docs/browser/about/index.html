About Us

Renovation Tech Robotics and Aeromodelling an organization formed by the students of Lovely Professional University. It works in the field of technology to whip up innovative ideas, with proper implementation and organizing events with regard to Robotics and Aeromodelling. We provide a dynamic environment for the students to execute their ideas in groups to forge real time application and apply them to manage practical situations so as to nurture existing skills to a competitive level.
